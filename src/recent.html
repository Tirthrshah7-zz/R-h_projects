7 Jabiru

7 Jabiru Drive, Fern Bay, NSW 2295

Meticulously crafted by multi-award winning Eden Brae Homes, this c.2013 single level abode is designed to embrace light, space and a seamless in/outdoor lifestyle complete with impressive alfresco entertaining and saltwater pool.

This home is spacious, with three large living zones plus a formal and informal dining spaces, giving ultimate flexibility for the modern family. Perfectly positioned on a sizable 798m2 level block, this allows garaging for 3 cars, plus additional driveway space, making this the perfect family home.
